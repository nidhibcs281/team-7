Business Cases Aligned with SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

1. Providing Wi-Fi Networks in Public Spaces Offering Affordable Internet Access

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
  • 11.2.1 - Proportion of urban population with access to affordable and sustainable transport systems, by sex, age, and persons with disabilities.

    • Target: By 2030, provide access to safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable transport systems for all, enhancing inclusive and sustainable urbanization.

  • 11.7.1 - Proportion of the urban population that has access to green spaces and public services, including affordable Wi-Fi access.

    • Target: Support the development of accessible public spaces with universal internet access and improve service delivery.

Data Structures and Algorithms:
  • Hashing: For efficient lookup and management of users and their devices across different public Wi-Fi access points.
  • Dijkstra's Algorithm: To optimize the distribution of internet traffic, ensuring strong connectivity across urban areas.

2. Unmanned Machines for Urgent Supplies and Disaster Information

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
  • 11.5.1 - Number of deaths, missing persons, and persons affected by disasters, including floods, heatwaves, and droughts.

    • Target: Significantly reduce the number of deaths and those affected by disasters by enhancing resilient infrastructures and urban systems.

  • 11.b.2 - Proportion of local governments that adopt and implement local disaster risk reduction strategies in line with national disaster risk reduction strategies.

    • Target: Integrate disaster risk reduction policies and strategies into urban planning to prepare better for disasters, such as floods, heatwaves, and earthquakes.

Data Structures and Algorithms:
  • BST or AVL Trees: For organizing disaster response routes or communication networks, optimizing travel for supply drops.
  • Kruskal's Algorithm: Managing connected components for identifying reachable areas in an affected city for emergency operations.

3. Improving Digital Payment Systems for Small Businesses

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
  • 11.3.2 - Proportion of cities with a direct contribution to job creation, entrepreneurship, and digital payment systems for small businesses in urban areas.

    • Target: Promote inclusive and sustainable urban economic growth through facilitating accessible financial systems and services for all businesses.

  • 11.6.1 - Percentage of urban population with access to public financial services such as mobile payments, supporting MSMEs in cities.

    • Target: Improve urban waste management, public financial literacy programs, and empower urban MSMEs with digital tools for long-term development.

Data Structures and Algorithms:
  • Hashing/Hash Functions: Ensuring secure and efficient lookups and payments in the transaction system for urban businesses.
  • Merge Sort: Sorting transaction data to ensure streamlined and quick processing of financial systems to support urban businesses.
