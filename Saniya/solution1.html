1.Coolaborative funding

SDG: 11

Target: 11.4

Indicator: 11.4.1

You are optimizing investment allocation across industries in the space sector to maximize Return on Investment (ROI) within a fixed budget.

1. Merge Sort: Sorts the projects by ROI in descending order, ensuring that the most impactful projects are considered first.

Efficiency: O(n log n)

2. Greedy Algorithm: Allocates the budget to projects with the highest ROI as long as the budget allows.

3. Bellman-Ford Algorithm: Simulates optimal resource allocation by considering debts and subsidies as edges with weights. Negative weights model the potential debt or subsidies effectively.

Efficiency: O(VE)
