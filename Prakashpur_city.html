Located in Shivamogga, Karnataka, Prakashpur is a smart city that combines modern innovation with the charm of tradition. Designed with sustainability in mind, it offers green energy, smart infrastructure, and eco-friendly solutions, all aimed at creating a better life for its residents. Prakashpur is where the future meets community, built to thrive for years to come.

City Layout Plan:

  • Central Business District (CBD): 100 sq. km
  • Residential Areas: 750 sq. km
  • Green Spaces: 250 sq. km
  • Industrial Zone: 150 sq. km
  • Transportation Infrastructure: 150 sq. km
  • Public Amenities and Utilities: 100 sq. km

Funding Sources:

  • 50% from Government (Central & State)
  • 30% from Public-Private Partnership (PPP)
  • 20% from International Institutes
