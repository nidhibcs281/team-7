Business Cases Mapped to SDG Targets

Smart Parking Solutions

Implementation

Smart parking solutions use IoT, sensors, and data analytics to optimize parking spaces in urban areas. This system enables efficient use of space, reduces traffic congestion, and enhances the urban mobility experience.

SDG Target: 11.2 - Provide access to safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable transport systems for all.

Indicator: Proportion of people with convenient access to parking facilities.

  • Utilizes smart sensors to detect available parking spots in real-time.
  • Provides app-based parking reservations, reducing time spent searching for parking.
  • Optimizes space utilization by guiding drivers to vacant spots.
  • Enhances urban mobility and accessibility through reduced congestion.

SDG Target: 11.6 - Reduce the environmental impact of cities.

Indicator: Reduction in traffic congestion and air pollution levels.

  • Minimizes the time spent searching for parking, reducing vehicle emissions and fuel consumption.
  • Reduces traffic congestion by streamlining parking processes and optimizing traffic flow.

Electric Public Transport

Electric public transport focuses on replacing traditional fuel-powered vehicles with electric vehicles (EVs), reducing carbon emissions, and contributing to a cleaner, greener city.

SDG Target: 11.2 - Provide access to safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable transport systems for all.

Indicator: Proportion of population with access to electric public transportation.

  • Encourages the use of clean energy through the adoption of electric buses and trams.
  • Reduces the carbon footprint of public transport systems by transitioning to renewable energy sources.
  • Improves urban air quality by reducing emissions from traditional vehicles.

Integration with City’s Central Control Systems

Integrating smart systems for transport, parking, and waste management with the city’s central control systems ensures coordinated, efficient urban management. This approach enhances decision-making and improves city-wide infrastructure performance.

SDG Target: 11.1 - Ensure access for all to adequate, safe, and affordable housing and basic services.

Indicator: Proportion of urban population with access to basic services through integrated systems.

  • Enables real-time monitoring and management of urban services, including transportation, parking, and waste collection.
  • Uses data analytics to optimize resource allocation and improve service delivery.
  • Promotes cross-sector collaboration for sustainable urban development.
